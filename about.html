Welcome to Hey Noodles!

Hey Noodles specializes in Xiăo Miàn, a beloved noodle dish from Chongqing, China. After the Xiăo Miàn craze swept through China, the first North American location opened in Toronto’s Chinatown in 2015.

Today there are multiple locations across the GTA.

Discover Chóngqìng Xiăo Miàn Flavours!

Chóngqìng Xiăo Miàn, or “Little Noodles,” is a traditional street food from Chongqing. Known for its numbing spice, wheat noodles, and aromatic toppings, this dish has gained popularity globally in cities such as Paris, New York, San Francisco, and Toronto.
